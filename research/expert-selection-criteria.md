# Expert Selection Criteria

Topic: LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

Experts were selected based on the following criteria:

1. Demonstrated experience growing B2B SaaS brands, products, or personal brands through LinkedIn.
2. Consistent publication of educational content.
3. Evidence of practical execution rather than purely theoretical advice.
4. Active presence on LinkedIn and/or YouTube.
5. Recognition within the B2B marketing community.
6. Content focused on organic growth, audience building, demand generation, or content distribution.

The goal is to collect high-signal sources that can later support the creation of a practical LinkedIn growth playbook for B2B SaaS companies.

  2. **The Non-Weird Relationship Playbook:** Use those AI-synthesized insights to build native LinkedIn touchpoints. Instead of pitching via cold DMs, AEs should publish hyper-specific POVs addressing those researched bottlenecks, utilizing existing company content but adding their own authentic technical perspective to remain top of mind for target buyers.

### AI-Native Tactical Insight
- **Core Pivot:** Eliminating expensive, highly scripted corporate video productions to implement a decentralized, low-fidelity video model that utilizes authentic human assets to resolve sales objections and drive pipeline velocity.
- **B2B SaaS Application:** For 100Hires, burning time and budget on polished brand videos is a legacy play with low returns. To operationalize McEwen's framework, the growth and sales teams should deploy an agile, multi-tier video playbook:
  1. **The Inbound FAQ Loop:** Mine recent sales calls and customer success data to isolate the top 5 technical objections raised by recruiters (e.g., "How does the platform prevent bias in automated candidate screening?"). Instead of writing a long PDF guide, have the founder or product lead record an unscripted, 60-second native video walking through the actual software dashboard. This builds immediate clarity and authority directly in the LinkedIn feed.
  2. **Video-Driven Social Selling:** Train Account Executives (AEs) to abandon text-heavy cold outreach and rigid follow-up templates. Implement a routine where AEs record personalized, 45-second native LinkedIn video messages or quick loom recordings addressing a specific prospect's hiring bottlenecks. This humanized delivery cuts through inbox noise, accelerates trust, and bypasses corporate gatekeepers.

### AI-Native Tactical Insight
- **Core Pivot:** Navigating the friction of untrackable digital journeys by replacing strict, link-dependent attribution models with a dual strategy of zero-click native content saturation and high-retention owned database capture (email).
- **B2B SaaS Application:** Since talent acquisition executives and buyers now consume insights and research tools inside walled gardens or conversational LLMs without executing traditional clicks, the growth engine at 100Hires must restructure its capturing mechanism:
